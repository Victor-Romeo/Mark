Social Issue: Racial Profiling / Terrorism.

For our social issue we chose to create an experience centered around racial profiling with a special emphasis on national security. We wanted to paint a clear image of the stress of being in the position of the shotcallers in our nations defense, and more importantly have users empathize with the stress that these individuals go through.

Racial Profiling : Tensions Examined

We live in a stressful time and topics like immigration, terrorism, and homeland security are often intertwined with age old struggles like racial profiling. Advocates argue that is a needed measure to ensure prompt and speedy response, whereas opponents argue that the ends can never justify such drastic means. We found several studies that reported an increase in the targeting of Arab and Muslim Americans as a result of terrorist attacks abroad. The seminole report we found was from Brown University. It is this high stress level environment that we sought to put the user into

Experience Design

In order to handle such a complex topic we designed an experience that has many twists and turns that are tailored based on your accelerated future choices. The flow chart below is a nice refernece for how the experience is designed to work. The first scene is a simple test, do you value collecting information or time as your main goal? The second scene is a self report newscast that will inform you on the implications of your choices. Lastly we put you in the hot seat to solve a high pressure sitation. We are purposely being slightly vauge so that you can discover the twists and turns yourself
